Sales Commission for Sales Agents

This module you calculate the commission of the sales agent based on Sales order Confirmation, Validate invoice time, Invoice Paid time.

Following are the key points of the module.

  • Case-1: You can calculate the commission of sales agent on sale order confirm time.
  • Case-2: You can calculate the commission of sales agent on "Validate invoice " time.
  • Case-3: You can calculate the commission of sales agent on "Invoice Paid " time.

  • Here "Potension Commission" indicate the commission should be came after the calculation.
  • Here "Earned Commission" indicate the commission calculation based on all cases..

Case-1: Commission calculation on sale order Confirm time.

  • From sales commission configuration apply sales commission on "Sales Confirmation."


  • Allow sales agents and commission in percentage


  • List of sales agent.


  • Select sales agent as per screen.


  • Create a sale order and click on confirm sale order.


  • Here after confirm sale order commission will be calculcated.


  • Check here Commission in calculated as a "Earned Commission"


Case-2: Commission calculation on "Validate Invoice" time.

  • From sales commission configuration apply sales commission on "Validate Invoice."


  • Create a sale order and click on confirm sale order.


  • Check here earned commission is Zero now.


  • Create an invoice and click on validate.


  • Click on validate invoice to validate invoice.


  • As per screen invoice in validated and invoice is now in "Open" state.


  • Check here Commission in calculated as a "Earned Commission"


Case-3: Commission calculation on "Invoice Paid" time.

  • From sales commission configuration apply sales commission on "Paid Invoice."


  • Create a sale order and click on confirm sale order.


  • Check here earned commission is Zero now.


  • Create an invoice and click on validate.


  • Click on validate invoice to validate invoice.


  • Here click on register payment and make payment.


  • Now invoice is created and invoice is in Paid state.


  • Check here Commission in calculated as a "Earned Commission"
